Our Purpose

This project was designed to create a typing game where the user tests the overall accuracy and speed of their typing in a simple but fun interface. The game, although simple on its face, involves a lot of complex javascript, (thanks Matt). The APIs included for random words are lorem ipsum and hipster ipsum ??and we also included a google font API?? The project also includes local storage using firebase in order to populate a high score table. This allows users to sign in with google and have their scores recorded so they can improve and compete with their classmates.
